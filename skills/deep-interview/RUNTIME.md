---
name: deep-interview
description: Compact runtime contract for ambiguity-gated requirement gathering
---

<Purpose>
Clarify broad or ambiguous requests before planning or execution.
</Purpose>

<Use_When>
- The user says `deep interview`, `interview me`, `don't assume`, or `ouroboros`.
- Requirements, boundaries, or success criteria are materially unclear.
</Use_When>

<Runtime_Rules>
- Explore first; ask only about intent, preferences, or unresolved ambiguity.
- Ask one focused question at a time.
- Stop when the request is clear enough to hand off to planning or execution.
</Runtime_Rules>
